Cinnamon Roll

Description

These homemade cinnamon rolls are soft, fluffy, and filled with a rich cinnamon-sugar mixture, perfect for a special breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 0.5 cup (200 ml) sugar
  • 1 package dry yeast
  • 4 cups (200 ml) flour mixture
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon
  • 1 cup (200 ml) sugar
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 package vanilla
  • 6 tablespoons powdered sugar

Steps

1.To prepare the dough, mix half a cup of sugar, warm milk, and yeast.

2.Add the eggs and 3 tablespoons of butter.

3.Gradually add the flour and start kneading.

4.Cover and let it rise for an hour.

5.Roll out the risen dough on the counter.

6.Spread butter on the dough, sprinkle 1 cup of sugar and 2 tablespoons of cinnamon on top.

7.Then start rolling it into a log.

8.Cut the rolled dough into pieces about 2 fingers thick.

9.Place each piece on a baking tray.

10.Cover with a cloth and let it proof for 20 minutes.

11.Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 20 minutes.

12.In a bowl, mix cream cheese, 3 tablespoons of butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla.

13.Spread the frosting on the cinnamon rolls when they are warm from the oven.

14.Enjoy your homemade cinnamon rolls!
